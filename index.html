    A Collection of Classics

    Angustura Collins

  • Ingredients

    • 1 ½ oz Angustura bitters
    • 1 ½ oz lemon juice
    • 1 ½ oz soda water
    • ½ oz simple syrup
    • lemon peel to garnish

    Instructions

    Shake the lemon juice (1 ½ oz) and simple syrup (1 ½ oz) vigorously over ice. Strain into tom collins glass. Add soda water (1 ½ oz) and stir gently to combine. Float the Angustura (1 ½ oz) using a spoon. Finally, add lemon peel to garnish, optionally rubbing around the rim of the glass for a more zesty first sip.
